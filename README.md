## 🛠 Features

The tool includes several modules to assist your playstyle.

### Ball Predictor
The predictor draws a line based on the current arc of the ball. This helps you judge where the ball lands after contact with a wall or the floor. It updates in real time as the ball moves through the air.

### Aimbot
The aimbot adjust your camera view to center on the ball. It assists with recovery and alignment. You can toggle this feature on or off during a match.

### Boost Manager
This feature monitors your current boost levels. It shows a timer indicating how long your remaining boost lasts. It also highlights the nearest boost pads on your screen.

### ESP Indicators
ESP places visual tags on opponents and teammates. You see player names and their current location through obstacles. This helps you track the defense and position your car better for strikes.

## 🛡 Security and Safety

The tool functions as an external overlay. It reads game memory to calculate positions but does not inject code into the game files directly. This approach keeps your game files clean and prevents crashes. 

Keep your antivirus software updated. Some security systems flag custom tools because they perform memory scans. If your antivirus prevents the launch, add the RL-Precision-Tool folder to your exclusion list. This allows the software to read the necessary files to display the overlays.

The developer does not collect your login data or personal information. All calculations happen locally on your hardware. No data leaves your machine.

## 📈 Improving Performance

If you experience frame rate drops, decrease the refresh rate of the overlays in the settings menu. Lowering the refresh rate reduces the demand on your graphics card. Ensure your graphics drivers are up to date to maintain smooth performance.

You might also limit the number of active features. If you only need the ball predictor, turn off the ESP and aimbot modules. This frees up your system resources for the game itself.
